# ReadRise — Product Overview

**Last updated:** 2026-02-21
**Status:** Inception

---

## Vision

> "Know what you've read, understand how you read, and read more."

ReadRise is a reading companion SaaS for serious and casual readers. It is not just a book log — it is a system that helps readers build habits, understand their reading patterns, and stay motivated.

---

## Target Users

- People who read books regularly (physical, ebook, or audiobook)
- Goodreads users frustrated by its outdated UX and social-first approach
- Readers who want to understand their reading habits through data
- Habit-driven readers who want streaks, goals, and accountability

---

## Platforms

| Platform | Phase |
|----------|-------|
| Web app (responsive) | MVP |
| iOS native app | MVP |
| Android app | Phase 2 |

---

## Key Differentiators vs. Goodreads

| Goodreads | ReadRise |
|-----------|----------|
| Social-first | Privacy-first, social optional |
| No session timer | Built-in reading timer |
| No reading speed tracking | Tracks pages/hour automatically |
| Cluttered, dated UX | Clean, minimal interface |
| Weak goals system | Flexible goal engine |
| Stale recommendations | Personalized from your own data |

## Feature Areas

### 1. Library & Book Management
- Add books by search (title, author, ISBN)
- Barcode scan via iOS camera
- Import library from Goodreads (CSV)
- Organize into shelves: Reading, Want to Read, Finished, Abandoned, Re-reading
- Custom shelves and tags
- Series tracking
- Edition and format tracking (physical, ebook, audiobook)

### 2. Reading Progress Tracking
- Log progress by page, percentage, or chapter
- Set start and finish dates (or infer from sessions)
- Mark books as abandoned with optional reason
- Re-read support with separate progress per instance
- Reading streaks (daily habit tracking)

### 3. Reading Session Timer
- Start/stop session timer
- iOS background-aware timer
- Log sessions manually with duration and pages covered
- Auto-calculate reading speed (pages/hour)
- Session notes or highlights

### 4. Ratings & Reviews
- 1–5 star rating (half-stars optional)
- Private reading journal per book
- Optional public reviews on profile
- Tag emotions or themes (funny, dense, inspiring, slow-burn)

### 5. Reading Goals
- Annual reading challenge (X books per year)
- Pages-per-day goal
- Genre diversity goals
- Time-based goals (X minutes/day or week)
- Progress vs. pace visualization

### 6. Statistics & Insights
- Books read per month/year
- Total pages and hours read
- Average time to finish a book
- Reading speed trends
- Genre breakdown charts
- Best reading times of day
- Longest and shortest reads
- Year-in-Review summary (shareable card)

### 7. Discovery & Wishlist
- Want to Read queue with priority ordering
- Recommendations based on finished books
- Author follow with new release notifications

### 8. Social & Community (opt-in)
- Public reading profile
- Follow friends, see their currently-reading
- Reading challenges with friends
- Share Year-in-Review to social media

### 9. Goodreads Migration
- One-click import via CSV export
- Map Goodreads shelves to ReadRise shelves
- Preserve ratings and dates

### 10. Notifications & Reminders
- Daily reading reminder (customizable time)
- Streak protection alerts
- Goal pace alerts
- New release alerts for followed authors
